##### Features
* Along the wall to the right of the door, is a row of **8 large kegs**, 1ft radius. The kegs contain, (in order from the door to the far wall.) 
	* **Kegs 1, 2.** Ale
	* **Keg 3.** Mead
	* **Keg 4.** Red Wine
	* **Keg 5, 6.** Beer
	* **Keg 7.** Whiskey
	* **Keg 8.** [[Embrelead]]

	* **Wrapped.** fish, mutton, chicken
